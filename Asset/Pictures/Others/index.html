VHCIE@IEEEVR2021

VHCIE is a half a day workshop associated with the IEEE VR conference that aims at presenting state of the art character and crowd animation techniques for interactive characters, examples of new research opportunities by the availability of populated virtual environment, new research on avatars as well as discussing technological requirements for future applications.
